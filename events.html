UC Irvine Athletics

Athletics at the University of California, Irvine is to facilitate and enrich the education and personal growth of its students through their participation in competitive NCAA Division I athletics. Intercollegiate Athletics is committed to the welfare of student-athletes and staff, and advocates an environment that promotes excellence in athletic and academic performance, sportsmanship, diversity, and gender equity.

Bren Events Center

An elegantly designed, modern arena on the campus of the University of California, Irvine, the Bren Events Center provides an exciting and prestigious location in Orange County for a full array of musical, performance, exhibition, sporting, and other public events.

Irvine Barclay Theatre

An intimate venue with first-class talent, Irvine Barclay Theatre showcases top-notch artists and entertainers from across the nation and around the world.
